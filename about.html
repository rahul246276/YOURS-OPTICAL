Our Heritage

The Beginning

A Vision of Excellence

Founded in 1985 by a master optician with an unwavering passion for perfection.

What began as a small family-owned optical shop in the heart of Kolkata has blossomed into a premier destination for luxury eyewear. Our philosophy was elegantly simple: every pair of glasses should be a masterpiece of craftsmanship, combining optical precision with artistic beauty.

Over the decades, we have remained true to this founding vision. We traverse the globe to source the finest materials—Japanese titanium, Italian acetate, German optical glass—and collaborate with master artisans who share our relentless commitment to excellence.

Today, Yours Optical stands as a testament to our unwavering dedication to quality, innovation, and personalized service. We continue to curate the world's finest eyewear collections while maintaining the intimate, client-focused experience that has been our hallmark since day one.
